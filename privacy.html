Privacy Policy for All in One Sport

Last Updated: 11 Sept, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to All in One Sport! This privacy policy is designed to inform you about the data we collect, if any, and how we use it when you use our Android app. We value your privacy and want to ensure that you have a clear understanding of how your data is handled.

Information We Don't Collect

At All in One Sport, we are committed to respecting your privacy. Therefore, we want to clarify that we do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Personal Information: We do not request or collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal details.
  • Location Data: We do not access or track your location through the app.
  • Device Information: We do not gather information about your device, such as the device model, operating system, or unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Information: We do not monitor or record your app usage behavior, including the features you use or the time spent within the app.
  • Third-Party Accounts: We do not request access to your social media accounts or any other third-party accounts.

Information We May Collect

While we make every effort to minimize data collection, we may collect non-personal, aggregated, and anonymized information for the purpose of app improvement and analytics. This information does not identify you personally and includes:

  • App Analytics: We may use third-party analytics services to collect information about how the app is used, such as the number of downloads, app crashes, and general usage patterns.

How We Use Information

The non-personal, aggregated, and anonymized information we collect, if any, is solely used for the purpose of improving the functionality and user experience of the All in One Sport app. This data helps us identify areas for enhancement and make informed decisions about future updates.

Third-Party Services

Please note that the app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites or services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies before interacting with them.

Children's Privacy

All in One Sport is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from individuals under the age of 13.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted within the app, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

Thank you for using All in One Sport. We hope you enjoy the app without any concerns about your privacy!
